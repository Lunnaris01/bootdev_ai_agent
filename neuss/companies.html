Companies in Neuss

Neuss is an important economic hub with a diverse range of companies:

  • Port of Neuss: One of Germany's largest inland ports, crucial for logistics and trade in the region.
  • 3M Deutschland GmbH: The German subsidiary of the global diversified technology company, with a significant presence in Neuss.
  • Kawasaki Motors Europe N.V.: The European headquarters of the Japanese motorcycle and engine manufacturer.
  • Rheinland Klinikum Neuss GmbH: A major healthcare provider in the region, operating several hospitals and medical centers.
