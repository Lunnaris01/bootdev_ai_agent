Diagon Alley

The bustling hub of the wizarding world.

Diagon Alley is a wizarding alley located in London. It is accessible to wizards through a secret entrance in the Leaky Cauldron pub. It is home to many shops and restaurants, including Ollivanders, Gringotts Wizarding Bank, and Weasleys' Wizard Wheezes.
