Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry

The finest school of witchcraft and wizardry.

Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry is the British wizarding boarding school, located in the Scottish Highlands. It is the primary setting for the first six books in J. K. Rowling's Harry Potter series. The school is divided into four houses, each bearing the last name of its founder: Godric Gryffindor, Helga Hufflepuff, Rowena Ravenclaw, and Salazar Slytherin.
