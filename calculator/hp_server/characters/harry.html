The Boy Who Lived

Harry Potter is the main character in J.K. Rowling's Harry Potter series. He is an orphan who discovers on his eleventh birthday that he is a wizard and has been invited to attend Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Known for his distinctive lightning-bolt scar and his strong sense of justice, Harry is destined to confront the dark wizard Lord Voldemort.

Throughout his years at Hogwarts, Harry forms strong bonds with his best friends, Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger, and faces numerous challenges, uncovering secrets about his past and the wizarding world. His bravery, loyalty, and selflessness are central to his character as he embraces his destiny.
